ABOUT US

We at CLEAN AURA are committed to providing accessible, safe, and clean hygiene solutions to everyone. Our mission is to enhance public health and safety by offering real-time restroom locators and a platform to review and rate hygiene standards. Join us in our journey to create a cleaner, healthier world.

OUR VALUES:

  • Accessibility: Ensuring hygiene solutions are available to everyone.
  • Safety: Providing safe and reliable information on hygiene facilities.
  • Community: Building a community that values and upholds cleanliness and health.
